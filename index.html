About Us

Our Team

As a close-knit family of innovative thinkers, their mission is to push the boundaries of aviation, focusing on continuous learning and knowledge sharing. In their pursuit of excellence, they proudly declare, "When everyone ran towards success, we chose to fly. That’s when we became wolves of the sky."

We are a motorsports team of Pimpri Chinchwad college of engineering which develops UAVs (unmanned ariel vehicles). Team maverick was established in 2021.

We compete nationally and internationally in Aero Design and emphasize on project management, teamwork and industry compliance.

Established in 1999, Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE) is a leading engineering institution in Pune, committed to providing a comprehensive and top-notch educational environment.

Recognized as the best private engineering college by ISTE, PCCOE boasts a renowned legacy, a top-tier placement cell in Maharashtra, and holds NAAC accreditation with a Grade, ensuring the development of socially devoted and technologically skilled engineers.

Our mission is to push the boundaries of what’s possible in the aviation sector.

We focus on developing UAVs: unmanned aerial vehicles according to the problem statement, we believe in learning and sharing.

The team's objective is to allow students to transcend themselves and sharpen their skills in the aero domain.

The team strives to be part of the expanding field and is devoted to building more promising aircraft in the future and contributing the society in terms of applications.
